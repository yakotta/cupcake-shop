Find our bakery!

We are located in the Desolation Wilderness, accessible via the Granite Lake Trail or the Pacific Crest Trail. From either trail, find the lightning-struck tree and follow the fossilized hoofprints into the tree, listeing for the sounds of our bone chimes and the smells of our oven.

Our previous location, off Prospectors Road near Coloma, is now permanently closed.

We are open year-round, sunset to sunrise.
